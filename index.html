Growth Associate Case Study

Inbound Lead Qualification for askLio's Enterprise Pipeline

A working system that enriches, scores, and routes enterprise procurement leads in real time. Built in Clay, connected to Slack and Attio.

Prepared by Lia Hofmann
Date March 2026
Tools Clay, Clearbit, Slack, Attio
01 ICP & Targeting
Who should askLio go after
askLio has proven traction with DACH enterprise industrial companies: TÜV SÜD, REHAU, Dormakaba, Burda, AL-KO.

Company Size

5,000 to 50,000 employees with €1B+ revenue. Large enough for complex indirect procurement, agile enough to buy without 12-month cycles.

Geography

DACH-first (Germany, Austria, Switzerland). Proven sales motion, German-language product, SAP-native, GDPR-compliant. Then EU expansion.

Industry Fit

Manufacturing, industrial, chemicals, energy, pharma — industries with high indirect spend and thousands of non-procurement requesters.

Buying Signals

New CPO or Head of Procurement just hired — first 6 months, mandate to modernize, actively evaluating tools.

Recently implemented P2P and disappointed — spent millions on SAP Ariba, procurement still takes weeks, actively searching for "what's next".

Competitor adopted AI procurement — board is asking "why aren't we doing this?"

Just saw askLio in press — BME award, YC announcement, conference appearance — timing-driven urgency.

Existing P2P system underdelivers — SAP Ariba, Coupa, or Jaggaer with high tail spend, understaffed teams.

Tier 1

Industrial Manufacturing, Professional Services, Media & Conglomerates

Mirrors existing customer profile. High indirect spend complexity.

Tier 2

Automotive Suppliers, Pharma & Chemicals, Energy & Utilities

Strong fit but longer sales cycles. Requires industry-specific case studies first.

Why not BMW or Siemens?

BMW built their own internal procurement AI (AIconic, 1,800 users). Siemens runs JAGGAER + Scoutbee + UiPath with 12+ month procurement cycles. The sweet spot is DACH Mittelstand: large enough to need procurement AI, agile enough to buy it.

Primary Persona

Head of Procurement / CPO / VP Purchasing

Buried in operational tasks, can't shift to strategic work. Has authority to sign.

Secondary Persona

Head of Digital / IT / Transformation

Evaluates and champions AI tools for procurement. Has budget and veto power on integrations.

02 Workflow Design
From demo request to AE call in under 60 seconds
Every inbound lead is enriched, scored, and routed automatically.
Inbound Trigger

Lead enters via demo form

Typeform captures first name, last name, phone, email. Response flows into Clay table automatically.

↓
Filter

Email type detection

Formula checks corporate vs. personal email. Personal emails are flagged to save enrichment credits.

→
Enrich

Clearbit company enrichment

Domain extracted from email. Returns company name, employee count, HQ country, industry. Only runs on corporate emails.

↓
ICP Scoring 0 to 5 Points

Automatic lead qualification

Every lead is scored against ICP criteria. Additive model so strong leads surface even with missing data.

+2 Employees 5,000+
+1 Employees 1K to 4,999
+1 HQ in DACH region
+1 ICP industry match
+1 Procurement or Digital title
↓
🔥 Hot Score 4-5

Slack alert fires instantly

Message includes company name, ICP score, phone number, and "Call within 1 hour" action.

⏳ Warm Score 2-3

Call within 24 hours

Lead enters Attio with full enrichment data. AE follows up with relevant case study.

❄️ Cold Score 0-1

Nurture or deprioritize

Entered into CRM but no immediate action.

↓
Action

AE calls the prospect

AE sees Slack notification, opens Attio for context, and calls within 1 hour. No answer? Lead goes into Lemlist follow-up sequence.

+
CRM

All leads to Attio

Every lead is pushed to Attio via REST API automatically.

· · ·
Edge Case

Personal email pending review

Gmail, hotmail, yahoo skip enrichment. Status: "Pending." Future: automated Lemlist reply asking "Which company are you with?" to re-enter scoring.

04 Future Evolution
Next steps
The core workflow is working. These are the next features to build:
1

Lemlist follow-up sequences

Warm leads who don't answer the phone get pushed automatically into Lemlist campaign. Email says: "Thank you for your interest, I tried to reach you, when are you available to talk?" with case study enclosed. Personal email leads get "Which company are you with?" automatically.

2

Attio analytics and closed-loop feedback

Track which lead scores convert to meetings and deals. Refine scoring weights based on conversion data.

3

Multi-channel aggregation

Unify LinkedIn DMs, phone inquiries, and event sign-ups into same Clay pipeline via webhooks.

4

Intent signals and automated re-entry

Add hiring signals, funding rounds, leadership changes. When personal email leads reply with company name, AI parses it and re-runs enrichment.
